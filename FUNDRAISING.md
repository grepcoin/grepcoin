# GrepCoin Fundraising & Launch Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Goal:** Raise $25,000-50,000 to fund GrepCoin's mainnet launch, security audit, and initial liquidity.

**Timeline:** 30-day campaign

**Approach:** Multi-channel fundraising combining traditional crowdfunding (Indiegogo) with crypto-native community presale.

## Fundraising Channels

### 1. Indiegogo Campaign

**Type:** Flexible Funding (keep what you raise)

**Duration:** 30 days

**Reward Tiers:**

| Tier | Price | Reward | Quantity |
|------|-------|--------|----------|
| Supporter | $10 | Discord role + name in credits | Unlimited |
| Early Gamer | $25 | 500 GREP tokens at launch + Supporter rewards | 500 |
| Bronze Backer | $50 | 1,200 GREP + exclusive NFT avatar + Early Gamer rewards | 300 |
| Silver Staker | $100 | 2,800 GREP + rare NFT + beta access + Bronze rewards | 200 |
| Gold Founder | $250 | 8,000 GREP + legendary NFT + founder badge + Silver rewards | 100 |
| Diamond OG | $500 | 20,000 GREP + 1-of-50 genesis NFT + governance rights + Gold rewards | 50 |
| Whale | $1,000 | 50,000 GREP + custom in-game character + all rewards | 20 |
| Arcade Partner | $2,500 | Your game featured on platform + 150,000 GREP + all rewards | 5 |

**Potential Raise:** $50,000-100,000 if all tiers sell

### 2. Crypto Community Presale

**Mechanism:** Direct ETH/USDC contribution via smart contract

**Price:** $0.005 per GREP (50% discount from planned launch price of $0.01)

**Allocation:** 5,000,000 GREP (5% of total supply)

**Min/Max:** $25 minimum, $2,500 maximum per wallet

**Vesting:** 25% at TGE, 75% linear over 3 months

**Smart Contract Features:**
- Whitelist-based (Discord members first)
- Referral bonus (10% extra GREP)
- Early bird bonus (first 48 hours: +20%)

### 3. GitHub Sponsors

**Tiers:**
- $5/mo - Supporter badge
- $25/mo - Early access to features
- $100/mo - Monthly call with team

## Marketing Strategy

**Goals:** Build anticipation, grow community to 1,000 Discord members

**Actions:**
- [ ] Launch Discord server with bot, roles, channels
- [ ] Twitter account activation (@grepcoin)
- [ ] Teaser video/GIF of gameplay
- [ ] "Coming Soon" landing page
- [ ] Reach out to 20 micro-influencers
- [ ] Submit to crypto news sites (CoinTelegraph, Decrypt)
- [ ] Reddit posts in r/CryptoGaming, r/PlayToEarn, r/ethereum

### Phase 2: Campaign Launch (Week 2-3)

**Goals:** Drive traffic to Indiegogo, hit 50% funding in first week

**Actions:**

- [ ] Email blast to subscribers
- [ ] Twitter Spaces AMA
- [ ] Discord launch event
- [ ] Influencer posts go live
- [ ] Product Hunt launch
- [ ] Hacker News "Show HN" post

- [ ] Referral competition

- [ ] Update posts with progress
- [ ] Cross-promotions with other projects
- [ ] Second wave influencer outreach

**Goals:** FOMO, hit stretch goals, convert fence-sitters

**Actions:**
- [ ] "48 hours left" campaign

| Regulatory issues | Clear utility token positioning, no profit promises |
| Token price crash | Vesting schedules, locked liquidity |
