### Our Solution

GrepCoin flips the script:

**Free to Play, Fun to Earn**
Jump into any of our 8 arcade games instantly. No wallet required to start. Earn GREP tokens based on skill, not investment.

**Sustainable Tokenomics**
Real revenue from cosmetics and tournaments funds rewards. No infinite minting. Built to last.

**Open Source & Community-Owned**
100% MIT licensed. Fork it, modify it, contribute to it. This is YOUR platform.

**Developer-Themed Fun**
Games built for the indie dev community: Grep Rails, Stack Panic, Bug Hunter, Regex Crossword, and more.

### The Games

**1. Grep Rails** - Build train tracks by matching regex patterns
**2. Stack Panic** - Clear function calls before they overflow the stack
**3. Merge Miners** - Navigate Git branches and collect merge rewards
**4. Quantum Grep** - Observe quantum particles and match patterns
**5. Bug Hunter** - Find and squash bugs in scrolling code
**6. Crypto Snake** - Classic snake with blockchain vibes
**7. Syntax Sprint** - Build valid code from falling syntax tokens
**8. RegEx Crossword** - Solve crossword puzzles with regex clues

### How It Works

1. **Connect your wallet** (or play as guest)
2. **Choose a game** from our arcade
3. **Play and earn** GREP tokens based on your score
4. **Stake your GREP** for up to 2x multipliers
5. **Compete** on leaderboards for bonus rewards
6. **Govern** the platform through DAO voting

### Risks & Challenges

**We believe in transparency:**

1. **Regulatory Risk:** Crypto regulations are evolving. We've structured GREP as a utility token with immediate use, but laws may change.

2. **Market Risk:** Crypto markets are volatile. GREP's value will fluctuate.

3. **Technical Risk:** Smart contracts can have bugs. That's why we're prioritizing a security audit.

4. **Adoption Risk:** We need players. That's why we're building community-first.

**Our mitigations:**
- Professional legal and security review
