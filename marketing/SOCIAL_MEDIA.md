## Content Strategy

### Content Pillars

1. **Product Updates (30%)** - New features, games, improvements
2. **Educational (25%)** - How crypto gaming works, tutorials
3. **Community (20%)** - Highlights, contests, UGC
4. **Behind-the-Scenes (15%)** - AI collaboration, development process
5. **Industry (10%)** - News, trends, commentary

### Posting Schedule

**Twitter:**
- 3-5 tweets per day
- Mix of original content, replies, retweets
- Best times: 9am, 12pm, 6pm EST

**Discord:**
- Daily announcements
- Weekly community updates
- Real-time engagement

## Community Management

### Response Guidelines

**Positive comments:**
- Thank them
- Ask follow-up questions
- Invite to Discord

**Questions:**
- Answer quickly and thoroughly
- Link to resources
- Invite to Discord for more help

**FUD/Negative:**
- Stay calm and professional
- Address legitimate concerns
- Ignore trolls
- Never argue

**Scam attempts:**
- Delete immediately
- Ban user
- Warn community

### Moderation Rules

1. No spam
2. No scams or phishing
3. No harassment
4. No NSFW content
5. No price speculation/financial advice
6. Be respectful
7. No impersonation
