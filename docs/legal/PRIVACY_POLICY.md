When you connect your cryptocurrency wallet to our Services, we collect:

- **Leaderboard Data:** Rankings, high scores, and competitive performance metrics

### 2.3 Technical Data

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, browser type and version

## 4. Blockchain Data and Public Information

### 4.1 Public Nature of Blockchain

**IMPORTANT:** Blockchain transactions are inherently public and transparent. When you stake GREP tokens, claim rewards, or conduct any on-chain transaction, the following data becomes permanently visible on the public blockchain:

- Your wallet address
- Transaction amounts and token balances
- Transaction timestamps and block numbers
- Staking tier, duration, and lock periods
- Reward claim history and amounts
- Smart contract interactions

### 4.2 Immutability

We **cannot** delete, modify, or hide on-chain data. This is a fundamental characteristic of blockchain technology, not a limitation of our Services. Once data is recorded on the blockchain, it is permanent and publicly accessible.

### 4.3 Blockchain Explorers

Anyone can view this information using blockchain explorers (e.g., Basescan, Etherscan). You should be aware of this public nature when using blockchain-based services.
